About

We are a clothing collective store in Harare that specializes in housing local & regional brands. We strive to provide our customers with quality service and clothing.

We sell womens clothing, that includes:

  • Tops & Fashion Tees
  • Bottoms & Pants
  • Jeans
  • Loungewear
  • Activewear
  • Shoes(Sandals, boots, heels etc)

Drop in our store for a visit at 1 Hurworth Road, Highlands, Harare, Zimbabwe
